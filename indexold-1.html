Design Changes.

I decided it was time for a rebranding of my personal website. The prior version was made by a younger, more naive self. Originally, I had planned to download a free bootstrap theme, to which I'd update the content to be relevant to me and my goals. Soon, however, I realized I didn't want to make my personal website completely based off of someone else's work. It was this desire that drove me back to the drawing board to think of a design that would be my own. This is the design you are seeing today.

I believe that design says a lot about a person's character and personality. This was one of the main reasons I decided scrap the bootstrap template website. The process for me in choosing this design went a little something like this:

1. I'm lazy so I'll just use someone else's work...nbd right?

2. Wait, what am I thinking, I have my own ideas, I can be original

3. *Think of design*

4. Implement design

I ended up using one of the most minimalistic bootstrap templates with a sidebar menu and proceeded to make it my own. For those who are interested, take a look at my github repo for this website.
